Pitbulls

Sad truth

Yes some pitbulls have been responsible for biting and attacking people but these are events relating to special circumstances. Lots of dogs bit people for many different reasons, and it is never the breed of dog that causes it. Treating a dog with love and respect raises a dog that loves and respects people.

Facts you should know

  1. They are very kind dogs.
  2. They can be trusted around children.
  3. They are full of energy, like crazy amonts of energy.
    • This means taking them out as often as you can and playing with them.
    • Do not just leave them alone or kenneled for hours on end, it is bad for their mentality and their health.
  4. As awesome as these dogs are they are also prone to some health disorders, always talk with your veternarian about these things.
